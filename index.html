Anagram Hunt

An anagram is a word or phrase formed by rearranging the letters, e.g. HUNT, by using each letter exactly once in the new word or phrase. An anagram is basically a play on words, often with a comedic or satiric intent. The letters of many words or phrases can be rearranged to form an anagram.

Math Facts Practice

Basic math facts are defined as computations involving the four basic math operations: addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division; using the single-digit numbers, 0 - 9. These basic facts are often referred to in current literature as “basic number combinations”.
